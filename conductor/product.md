# Initial Concept
Данное приложение позволяет пользователю импортировать текст и читать его в режиме «Предложение за предложением». Также оно позволяет упростить читаемое предложение или посмотреть перевод одного из слов или перевод фразы в этом предложении. Также оно предоставляет возможность пользователю узнать настроение этого предложения, что очень помогает при чтении текстов на иностранном языке.

# Product Definition

## Vision
Lettura-Facile is a language-learning reading assistant designed to make complex texts accessible. It breaks down text into manageable chunks, providing AI-powered simplification, translation, and sentiment analysis to help users grasp both the meaning and the tone of a foreign language text.

## Goals
- **Accessibility:** Make any imported text easy to read for language learners.
- **Linguistic Insight:** Provide deep linguistic support including word/phrase translation and sentiment detection.
- **Engagement:** Maintain user interest by simplifying difficult passages and focusing on sentence-level understanding.

## Target Audience
- **Language Learners:** People studying a new language who need help with authentic texts.
- **General Readers:** Anyone dealing with complex or technical documents in a foreign language.

## Core Features
1. **Sentence-by-Sentence Mode:** Focus on one sentence at a time to reduce cognitive load.
2. **Unified Display Modes:** A 3-state control to switch instantly between Original, AI-Simplified, and Translated versions of the current sentence.
3. **Interactive Word/Phrase Dictionary:** Instant translation for single words or multi-word phrases via direct interaction with the text.
4. **Sentiment Analysis:** Reveal the "mood" or tone of the current sentence to improve emotional comprehension.
5. **Text Import:** Support for pasting or uploading custom text for analysis.
6. **Background Synonym Extraction:** Automatic identification of complex words with contextual synonym generation for enhanced vocabulary support.

## User Experience Guidelines
- **Minimalist Interface:** Focus on the text with no distractions.
- **Contextual Actions:** Simplification and translation should be one click away.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Details about a sentence (sentiment, simplified version) only appear when requested.
