# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Minimalist and Direct. Use clear, concise language for all interface elements and AI-generated outputs.
- **Goal:** Minimize cognitive load for language learners.
- **Language:** The UI should be consistent and avoid jargon.

## Visual Branding
- **Aesthetic:** Clean and Focused. Prioritize high-readability fonts and ample whitespace.
- **Color Palette:** Low-distraction colors (neutral backgrounds, subtle highlights for sentiment analysis).
- **Iconography:** Use simple, universally understood icons for translation and simplification actions.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Strategy:** Unified Stable Layout. Maintain a consistent fixed-viewport experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop. Ensure the PWA experience is smooth and offline-capable.
- **Interactions:** Contextual Hover/Click. Users should be able to tap/click on a word or sentence to see its translation or simplification without leaving their current place in the text.
- **Simplicity:** One core action per screen (Read, Simplify, Translate).

## Design Principles
1. **Consistency:** All linguistic aids (translation, mood, simplification) must follow the same interaction pattern.
2. **Speed:** AI responses should be fast and integrated directly into the reading flow.
3. **Accessibility:** Follow WCAG guidelines for contrast and font sizes to support all readers.
